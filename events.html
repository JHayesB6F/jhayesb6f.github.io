Big night out


Latest films from Hollywood? Check!   Independent cinema at great prices? Of course!   A great way to spend a rainy day? Naturally!   Brookstead's newly opened VUE Cinema showcases brand new films at prices much lower than London.

We also have a thriving arts industry, shown through the many powerful shows hosted by the Kenneth More Theatre. Recent additions such as Made In Dagenham: The Musical have come to Brookstead on tour every year since 2009, with performances getting better each time. And best of all? You can enjoy amazing nights out here in Brookstead for much cheaper then the West End - how's that for a bargain?
