Points of Interest


Brookstead is home to many different unique buildings and sites steeped in history, from food markets to carpet shops; so much so that we have been named SECRET SE Magazine's #1 emerging town of 2023. Everything is just waiting to be discovered!

Centre of Attention


Brookstead has never shyed away from the camera! For over 50 years, we've had a bustling independent film industry regenerating our economy! Most films such as Brookstead 98 document the struggles faced by everyday people living in Brookstead such as gang violence and drug misuse. Of course, since the film was released, more and more tourists are flocking to Brookstead to embrace our cultural heritage and unique history.

Some of our streets have also made the big screen! Brick Lane, for example, featured in Brookstead 98 during the chase sequence and riot soon after, fans of the film visit the street like a pilgramige every year! Also, the High Street has been featured in many news reports and documentaries about the history of the town and how it has contributed to Essex as a whole! If you want the whole experience, we have guided tours available year-round
