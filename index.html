Welcome to Brookstead


We are the nation's fastest growing large town in the southeast. Built up extensively over the last decade, we have a rich background of brick firing, manufacturing and an emerging arts industry to appeal to young talent. We have recently become home to many new small businesses making waves nationally; some even featured on the hit BBC show Dragon's Den!

Why visit us 

Brookstead is one of the country's fastest growing areas for tourism - our rich heritage is only part of our success.
