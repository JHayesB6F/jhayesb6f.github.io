Refresh your mind


Parks and green space are scattered throughout Brookstead. You'll find relaxing green spaces everywhere to suit your every need, ranging from bustling central parks to peaceful escapes just outside town. All of our parks have catering facilities open every day of the week, with some offering different menus depending on the day. Why not get some fresh air and wind down?                

Something for everyone


Brookstead consistently ranks in the top 3 yearly in the Essex in Bloom competition; no wonder as we have a long reaching horticultural history. In the 19th century, many former gardeners from Kew would travel to Brookstead for charity work, landscaping the town to prevent floods and anti-social behaviour. We also pioneered special face masks that reduce the effects of pollen in the summer months, so hayfever sufferers can still enjoy our fabulous gardens! Make sure to bring a jacket though; it's always windy over here!
