Our food scene


Food in our town is as varied as it gets. Many people from diverse cultural backgrounds call Brookstead home, and with them comes a passion for good food. You'll find everything here from lamb koftas to kosher bagels through our streets, all at resonsable prices. Why don't you come and try something new when you visit?      

Food and Brick Lane


Brick Lane is our iconic food quarter in Brookstead. The street as we know it today has been shaped by generations of immigrants from all over the world; some left London to find more peace and economic opportunity in an emerging market. To bounce back from the pandemic, we are offering discounts off the final bill by presenting a valid National Rail ticket to North Fambridge / Althorne to help boost our local economy. Offer valid until September 7th 2023.
